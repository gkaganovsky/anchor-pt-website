Your Therapist

Greg Kaganovsky,
DPT

Doctor of Physical Therapy Sports Rehabilitation Concussion Specialist Orthopedics

I coached competitive youth soccer for 12 years, and my path toward becoming a Physical Therapist began when one of my players fell into a seizure. In that moment, I realized I lacked the health knowledge and true understanding of the human body to be of real assistance — or a great mentor — to my students.

That experience sparked a pursuit that led me to earn my Doctorate in Physical Therapy. As a practicing therapist for over 3 years, I'm motivated to help the Ventura community with body maintenance so that people can continue to enjoy all the things they love to do.

I believe our bodies are important gateways to having a healthy and strong life. Whether you're recovering from injury or simply want to move better — I'm here to be your anchor.
